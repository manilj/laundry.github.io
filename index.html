How it works

Schedule your pickup

Use our calendar link to schedule a pick-up time. At the scheduled time we will pick-up your laundry load.

Choose your preferences

At the time, mention your preferences of washing (fabric softener etc.) and schedule a delivery time. Our price is $1.99/lb with a $20.00 minimum. Check our pricing area for more details.

Delivery

Once we are done with the pickup, your laundry will be washed, folder and delivered in 2-3 working days.

Commercial Laundry

Applies only to AirBnB and Hotel Laundry. Including washing, drying, folding, pick up and deliveries of bed sheets, pillow cases, towels and other laundry used by the guests.
